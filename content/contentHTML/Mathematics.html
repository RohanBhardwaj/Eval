Mathematics

Mathematics is the language of science. And every aspiring engineer is expected to achieve fluency in it. In IIT JEE , 30 questions out of a total of 90 , worth 120 marks come from the maths section. It is scoring if one is proficient in it , or else it can act as the tie-breaker.
