Physics

Physics is the heart and soul of any engineering entrance exam especially IIT JEE. Any student wishing to pursue engineering has to obtain mastery over this subject in order to score enough marks to surpass the cut off of IIT JEE Main. Out of a total of 90 questions in JEE Main, 30 questions of a net worth of 120 marks are from physics.
